About Us

St. Albans Daycare was established in 1974. We are a non-profit society that is administrated by a board of directors, operated by St. Albans Anglican Church. We provide a fun and exciting learning environment, in which children learn through play. We are in contact with other agencies, to provide each child with the support and help that they may need. We teach weekly topics through stories, songs and hands on experiences. Activity areas include: pre-reading, pre-writing, math, science, creative and social skills.

Awards

  • 2008, 2009, 2010 --- Burnaby Now Best of Burnaby Award - Best Daycare
  • 2007 --- BC Centre for Ability Community Partner Award
  • 2003-2004 --- Prime Minister's Award for Excellence in Early Childhood Education
  • 1999 --- BC Centre for Ability In Recognition Award
